╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║  FIX: Configure Your Power Automate Flow                     ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

STEP 1: Go to Power Automate
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
URL: https://make.powerautomate.com
Click: "My flows"


STEP 2: Find/Edit Your Flow  
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Look for: Workflow with HTTP trigger
          (using your webhook URL)
Click: Edit


STEP 3: Add Teams Action
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Click: "+ New step"
Search: "Post message in a chat or channel"
Select: Microsoft Teams connector


STEP 4: Configure Teams Action
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Post as:    Flow bot
Post in:    Channel
Team:       [Select your team - "NewAPI Team"]
Channel:    [Select your channel]
Message:    
            **@{triggerBody()?['title']}**
            
            @{triggerBody()?['text']}


STEP 5: Save and Test
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Click: Save
Run:   .\test_teams_simple.ps1
Check: Your Teams channel!


╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║  QUICK CHECK                                                 ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Go to Power Automate and check:

□ Is your flow enabled (turned ON)?
□ Does it have a Teams "Post message" action?
□ Does the Teams action have the correct channel selected?
□ Check "28-day run history" - do you see successful runs?
□ Click a run - does the Teams action show success or error?

1. Edit your flow
2. Click "Test" (top right)
3. Choose "Manually"
4. Provide test data:
   {
     "title": "Test Message",
     "text": "Hello from Power Automate!"
   }
5. Click "Run flow"
6. Check your Teams channel

If this works → Your flow is correct
If this fails → Check Teams permissions/channel selection
